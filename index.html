It’s time to book your seat

Biblus, one of NIE IEEE Student Branch's signature events of the year, is a National Level Paper Presentation Competition. The event follows the same format as that of the IEEE Standards.

Participation in teams of maximum 3 members.

The selection of abstracts and papers follow a strict plagiarism and scrutinizing procedure

Register



Round 1

Abstract Submission

 

Participants have to submit a brief overview of their innovative ideas within the deadline.

 

Round 2

Paper Submission

 

The participants selected for this round must submit their final papers in accordance with the abstract submitted by them.

Round 3

Paper Presentation

The participants selected for the final round must present their papers before a panel of judges, in the form of an oral presentation or interactive presentation.
